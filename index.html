Welcome to the Gems Crystal Game!

You are given a random number at the start of the game. This is your target score.

There are 4 crystals below. by clicking on a crystal, you add a number of points to your Total Score.

You win the game by matching the total score and the random number shown below, and lose if your score goes above the number.

Challenge: The value of each crystal is hidden from you untIl you click on it! Each time the game starts, the crystals get new values.
